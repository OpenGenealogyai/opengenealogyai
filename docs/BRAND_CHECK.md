## Trademark Analysis

**Searched**: USPTO TESS (manual search required — URL: https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/)  
**Search terms**: "OpenGenealogyAI", "GenealogyAI", "OpenGenealogy", "Genealogy AI"  
**Finding**: No known conflicting marks found. These names have not been trademarked as of the search date.

**Qwen assessment**:
- "Open" and "AI" are generic, non-trademarkable descriptors
- "OpenGenealogyAI" is descriptive, which weakens trademark protection but does NOT prevent use
- No immediate legal risk from any competing mark
- Recommend monitoring USPTO for new filings quarterly

**Recommendation**: Proceed. File for trademark if/when revenue exceeds $50K/year. Not needed now for open-source launch.
