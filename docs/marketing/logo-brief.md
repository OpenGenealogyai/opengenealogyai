# Logo Brief — OpenGenealogyAI

**Project:** OpenGenealogyAI
**Domain:** opengenealogyai.org
**Audience:** Genealogical researchers, open-source developers, AI/ML community
**Tone:** Scholarly, trustworthy, quietly technical — NOT corporate, NOT playful

---

## Brand Essence

OpenGenealogyAI sits at the intersection of:
- **Historical scholarship** — archival, serious, document-based
- **Open-source software** — transparent, collaborative, community-driven
- **Probabilistic reasoning** — honest about uncertainty, data-first

The logo must communicate honesty and depth. This is not a "fun genealogy app." It is an open standard for machine-readable human history.

---

## Visual Direction

### Primary Direction: The Branching Document

A family tree branch that resolves into a confidence bar or probability distribution at its tips — visually showing that the tree "ends in uncertainty" rather than false certainty.

Possible executions:
- A minimal tree branch (2-3 nodes) where the leaf nodes show `[0.82]` `[0.61]` labels
- A document/manuscript page with a branching line overlay
- A tree where the branches are slightly transparent/faded to suggest probabilistic weight

### Secondary Direction: The Archive Mark

A simple monogram or seal-style mark:
- Octagonal or circular border suggesting an archival stamp
- "OG·AI" or "OpenGenealogyAI" in a legible serif or slab serif
- Subtle compass rose or document fold in the background

### Rejected Directions (do not pursue):
- DNA helix (overused in genealogy)
- Generic family silhouettes (corporate/stock-art feel)
- Bright/colorful/cheerful aesthetic (wrong tone)
- Circular icons that look like social media logos

## Colors

**Primary palette:**
| Name | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Archive Night | `#1a1a2e` | Primary ink/background |
| Grove Green | `#2d6a4f` | Accent, confidence bars |
| Parchment | `#faf7f0` | Light backgrounds |
| Document Gold | `#b5873d` | Highlights, links |

**Avoid:** Bright blue, red, orange, neon colors of any kind.

---

## Typography

**Logo wordmark:** Libre Baskerville or similar humanist serif. Small caps for "OPEN", full caps for "GENEALOGY", small for "AI". OR a single weight in full caps with slightly wider tracking.

**Icon:** Should work as a standalone favicon at 16×16px in the Archive Night color on a Parchment background.
