Common Questions

What is a research action?

One agent step: searching a record collection, scoring a candidate match, cross-checking a date, or pulling a church record. A typical ancestor investigation uses 10–30 actions. Your free report uses about 14.

Do unused actions expire?

Half of your unused monthly actions roll over to the next billing cycle. They expire after 60 days.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal before your next renewal and you will not be charged again. Actions you have already purchased remain usable through the end of your billing period.

What payment methods are accepted?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. No PayPal or crypto at this time.

Can I add private document storage?

Yes — add it separately for $2/month (up to 1,000 documents), $5/month (up to 5,000), or $12/month (up to 20,000 items). Contact us after purchasing a bundle.
