Free Conversion

Convert Your Tree to MaxGen

If your tree is in GEDCOM, Ancestry, FamilySearch, or another format, we'll convert it to the open MaxGen standard — free of charge. AI-assisted conversion preserves every individual, date, and source note, and adds confidence scores to uncertain relationships so nothing gets lost in translation.

MaxGen trees are fully indexed by our search engine, making every surname and record discoverable to the community.

Why Share Your Tree?
🔍

Help Others Find Their Ancestors

Your research may be the missing link for someone else. A name you documented in passing could unlock decades of another family's search.

💬

Get Feedback & Corrections

Researchers who share their trees consistently report receiving corrections, new sources, and DNA matches they never would have found working alone.

🔒

Your Tree Stays Yours

We don't copy or host your data. Your tree lives wherever you put it — your GitHub, your Dropbox, your own site. We just list the link.

🤖

MaxGen Trees Get AI-Indexed

Trees in MaxGen format are crawled by our AI and become fully searchable by surname, place, date range, and record type across the entire community directory.
